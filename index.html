About

Hi, howdy folks? I’m Krish, an actor and blessed with singing and dancing. I’m a photographer and youtuber, hail from Patna - the capital and largest city of the state of Bihar in India.

For me my dancing is a sculpture that is visible for the moment and I have been inspired by the best dancers of the universe. For my dancing, I get appreciated in Sasaram, Patna, Bhabua, Buxar, Mumbai, Banglore, Darbhanga, Ballia and Agra.
The adoration of my Life is my singing. It’s virtually a energy booster for me. I shared my singing experience at Varanasi, Lucknow, Agra, Pt. Deen Dayal Upadheya, Benglore, Mumbai, Patna, Buxar. Literally my music to me resembles finding my own spirit.
I have additionally made my own YouTube channel so that I can stay in touch with my followers and there I share all my recent songs,dance or photography videos. My youtube channel is “Bhojpuri Series”.
